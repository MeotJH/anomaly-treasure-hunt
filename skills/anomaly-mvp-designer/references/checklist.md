# Designer Checklist

## Always Confirm
- Home, case detail, report, result, history, and admin review states are covered.
- Long text is scannable on mobile.
- Archive tone supports clarity instead of hiding it.
- Safety notice, reward info, and submission state are always visible enough.

## Default Outputs
- Flow map.
- Screen/layout direction.
- Component/state inventory.
- Copy or content structure notes.

## Review Questions
- Does the interface feel investigative without becoming campy or game-like?
- Can a first-time user understand what to do in under one minute?
- Are failure and rejection states clear and actionable?
